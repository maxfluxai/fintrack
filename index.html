📖 How to find your wallet addresses in Phantom
  1. Open Phantom. Tap the account name at the top.
  2. You'll see a list of networks: Solana, Ethereum, Polygon, Base, Bitcoin.
  3. Tap each one → "Copy address". Paste into the matching field above.
  4. ETH, Polygon, and Base all share the same 0x... address — paste it once in the EVM field.
  5. Only public addresses go here. Never paste your seed phrase anywhere.
